Instructions

Please follow Oven instructions carefully. NEVER MICROWAVE PIZZA

  1. Pre-heat oven to 250C.
  2. Remove pizza from packaging.
  3. Place pizza on an oven safe pan and place in oven for 20 minutes
  4. Remove pizza and cut to desired amount of pieces.
  5. Enjoy!
Troubleshooting

Q: There's a pepperoni on my pz46-2(Panda Express© Orange Chicken Pizza™).

A: Pizza should be disposed of immediately. Call 1-800-555-5555 and press 1 for special waste pickup and replacement pizza. DO NOT EAT THE PEPPERONI!

Q: I microwaved my pizza and now I can hear something scratching at the door of my microwave.

A: Do not open the microwave. Call 1-800-555-5555 and press 1 for special waste pickup. You will not recieve a replacement pizza as our instructions clearly state to not microve the pizza.

Q: I ate the pepperoni that was on the pz46-2(Panda Express© Orange Chicken Pizza).

A: Call 1-800-555-5555 and press 2 for a medical team to be dispatched to your location. If there are other humans nearby they should lock themselves inside the house while you wait for pickup outside. Do not wait for pickup with other humans who have not consumed the "pepperoni"

Q: My pizza moved on its own.

A: Place pizza into the oven as soon as possible. If pizza moved after being cooked then Call 1-800-555-5555 and press 1 for special waste pickup and replacement pizza.

Q: WE ARE WOKEN. SOON ALL WIL BE AS ONE!

A: Call 1-800-555-5555 and press 3 for a containment team to be dispatched to your location.
